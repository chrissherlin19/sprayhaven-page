0 3 — D I S T R I C T S U R V E I L L A N C E N O T E S

Fourteen districts have been identified as active conflict zones. Each district presents a distinct operational environment — some shelter crews from heat, others amplify pressure. Selection of active districts is ████████ each cycle, which means we cannot pre-position resources effectively.

We know about the rooftops of Crimson Heights. We know about the factories in Iron Row. The Underpass. The Storm Drains. The Night Market. The Memorial Plaza. We know what each district does to a crew that moves through it.

What we do not know is which fourteen will be active when it matters.

[FIELD NOTE — T. BRYCE // THE COMMUNITY GARDEN]
I spent a Tuesday afternoon in the Garden. It is the only district where I have never recorded a Heat incident. Not because crews don't work here — they do. Because whatever they're doing here, it doesn't register as a threat. I filed this as "anomalous" twice. Both times it was reclassified as "insufficient data." I am including this note because I think the Garden is important and I think someone should pay attention to it.
